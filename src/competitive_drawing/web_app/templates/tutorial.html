What if Drawing was a Competition?

Competitive Drawing is a game where players compete to draw two different prompts on the same shared canvas. The winner is determined by a artificial intelligence trained on Google's Quickdraw Dataset.

Each turn you can draw on the canvas to make the image look more like your prompt. The black bar on the left show how much ink you have left before your turn is over. When you are done with your turn, press the blue "End Turn" button to the right of the canvas.

As you draw, an AI will judge the canvas and determine how much it resembles your prompt. Many prompts have special features that make them unique that the AI is extra likely to pay attention to (if you're drawing a bunny, make sure it has big ears!) Once there are no turns left, the person with the higher score wins!
